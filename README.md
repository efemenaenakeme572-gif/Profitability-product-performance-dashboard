# Profitability-product-performance-dashboard
Power BI dashboard analyzing sales,profitability,and product performance

🧾 Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, and product-level insights.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly understand how different product categories and individual products contribute to overall business performance.

---

🎯 Business Problem
Businesses often struggle to:
- Identify which products are truly profitable
- Understand profit trends over time
- Distinguish between high sales and high profitability

This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing a clear and visual breakdown of financial performance and product impact.

---

📌 Key Metrics
- **Total Sales:** 104.54K  
- **Total Profit:** 9.19K  
- **Profit Margin:** 9%

---

📈 Dashboard Highlights

🔹 Profitability Insights
- Profit comparison across product categories  
- Monthly profit trend analysis  
- Clear visibility into high vs low profit areas  

 🔹 Product Performance
- Top 5 products by profit  
- Sales distribution by category (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies)  
- Identification of top contributors to revenue and profit  

🔹 Category Analysis
- Technology leads in both sales and profitability  
- Furniture shows moderate performance  
- Office Supplies generate lower profit despite strong sales  

---

🛠️ Tools & Skills Demonstrated
- **Power BI** – Dashboard design & data visualization  
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation  
- **DAX** – Measures (Profit, Profit Margin, KPIs)  
- **Data Analysis** – Trend identification & performance insights  

---

 💡 Key Insights
- High sales do not always translate to high profit  
- A small number of products drive a large portion of profit  
- Profitability fluctuates monthly, indicating seasonality or cost variation  
- Technology is the most profitable category
